Tomato Chutney

Must try...!

- Created on 21st July 2020


This is my special recipe which goes great with white rice and potato fry. Very simple recipe which tastes good with roti, idly, dosa and rice.
Let’s check out the recipe. :)


Ingredients

  • Tomatoes – 5 medium size
  • Garlic – handful (15 to 20 cloves)
  • Green chilly – 10 to 12
  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric – 1 tsp
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste


Instructions

  1. Chop green chillies, garlic and tomatoes.(Doesn’t need to be small as we will grind it later)
  2. Add oil in pan and heat it.
  3. Add mustard and cumin seeds.
  4. Add chopped green chili and garlic.
  5. keep the flame to medium low and fry them till the color about to change to brown.
  6. Add some coriander leaves and fry for a 30 sec.
  7. Shift this to a mixer jar.
  8. Add tomatoes and turmeric to the same pan and let them cook with lid closed, till they become soft and meshi.
  9. let the tomatoes cool down and then add them to the same mixer jar, add salt and grind them and then sift it to a bowl
  10. Now take pan add a tsp of oil, add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, curry leaves, dried red chili, 2 to 3 garlic cloves fry them and add it to our chutney.(if you don’t have some of these still our chutney taste great, not to worry)


Tips

Make sure you take equal amount of green chili and garlic; this makes the recipe tasty.
